One of my favorite things to do is cook. I also love to travel. One of my favorite things about traveling is the amazing food from other cultures and countries. While in Barcelona two years ago, I got to cook dinner with my host, his parents, and two of his friends. Being in the kitchen with so many different cultures' food and social practices was astounding. We had a big dinner together, then watched the neighborhood's elderly men play petanque until the early morning. Experiences like this one make life itself worth living.

Many people I meet find it odd that I prefer to travel alone. I tell them that I will make a friend wherever I go, so there's no need to take any with me! Traveling alone gives me the freedom to choose my own experience, whether it be to relax and undwind, or get lost in Paris for an entire day.
